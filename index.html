B.Sc. Thesis Work

The gate is losing
the argument

Three sheets, one gate, total control.

As channels shrink, the drain starts competing with the gate for control of the carriers. Wrapping the gate all the way around a stack of thin nanosheets is how the industry answers that, and it's what my B.Sc. thesis builds, calibrates and pushes on in Silvaco ATLAS.

My thesis partner Tanjida and I tune the gate stack, work function, inner spacers and stack configuration, then measure what each design choice costs in DIBL, electrostatic integrity and thermal stability, and benchmark the results against published GAAFET architectures.
