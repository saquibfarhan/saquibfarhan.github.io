Profile · Military Institute of Science & Technology

About

I'm an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate of the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Dhaka, where I finished with a CGPA of 3.92/4.00 and ranked first in the Electronics major.

My work sits at the device end of electronics. For my thesis I built and calibrated a 3D triple-stacked nanosheet GAAFET in Silvaco ATLAS, tuning the gate stack, work function and inner spacers, then measured what those choices cost in short-channel behaviour, electrostatic integrity and temperature stability. Around that, I've modelled AlGaN/GaN MIS-HEMTs and GaAs junctionless MOSFETs, designed low-power digital blocks in Cadence Virtuoso — ALUs, decoders, multiplexers and an FPGA logic block — and I'm currently designing a D-shaped photonic crystal fibre SPR biosensor in COMSOL.

A second thread of my research is applied deep learning for biomedical and sensor signals, which produced a Q1 IEEE Sensors Journal article on cross-subject driver drowsiness detection from single-channel EEG, plus conference work on cough classification, medicinal-plant recognition and neural-network PID control.

I also care a great deal about teaching. I designed and delivered a six-session Verilog HDL bootcamp, co-founded the IEEE Electron Devices Society chapter at MIST, and served as my department's student representative — undergraduate teaching, research and mentorship are where I want to keep working.

Selected projects · TCAD, Cadence, COMSOL

Design & simulation work

3D TCAD Optimization of a Triple-Stacked Nanosheet GAAFET

Silvaco TCAD

B.Sc. thesis

  • Built and calibrated a 3D triple-stacked nanosheet GAAFET in Silvaco ATLAS.
  • Optimised the device through gate-stack engineering, work-function tuning, inner-spacer design and stack-configuration analysis for better electrostatic control and drive performance.
  • Simulated short-channel effects (DIBL), electrostatic integrity and temperature stability to assess reliability.
  • Extracted small-signal parameters — transconductance, output conductance, gate capacitance — and benchmarked against reported state-of-the-art GAAFET architectures.

Modelling of an AlGaN/GaN MIS-HEMT Wide-Bandgap Transistor

Silvaco TCAD
  • Designed and simulated a 2D AlGaN/GaN MIS-HEMT, a wide-bandgap transistor suited to high-power and high-frequency communication systems.
  • Modelled the AlGaN/GaN heterostructure with polarisation-induced two-dimensional electron gas (2DEG) formation and a metal–insulator–semiconductor gate stack.

Configurable Logic Block for FPGA Applications

Cadence Virtuoso
  • Designed and verified a reconfigurable FPGA logic block in Cadence using LUTs and flip-flops to realise arbitrary combinational and sequential logic.

Performance Analysis of a GaAs DG-JLMOSFET

Silvaco TCAD
  • Analysed the electrical characteristics of a GaAs double-gate junctionless MOSFET, studying transfer and output curves under varying gate-oxide materials and thicknesses.

D-Shaped PCF-Based SPR Biosensor

COMSOL

Ongoing

  • Designing a highly sensitive D-shaped photonic crystal fibre (PCF) surface-plasmon-resonance biosensor in COMSOL Multiphysics, optimising structural parameters for sensitivity.
